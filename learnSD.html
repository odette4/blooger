Software engineers, often called software developers, plan and create programs. They determine user needs, design software and then test it to ensure quality. They give instructions to programmers who write the software code. Engineers who specialize in the operating systems needed by computers are called systems software developers. Those who focus on applications, such as games, video editors, word processors and databases, are called applications software developers.
